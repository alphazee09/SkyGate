About SkyGate

SkyGate is a comprehensive application designed to detect whether images and videos are AI-generated or authentic. The application leverages state-of-the-art detection algorithms to provide accurate and detailed analysis results.

As AI-generated content becomes increasingly sophisticated, the ability to distinguish between real and synthetic media is more important than ever. SkyGate addresses this challenge with multiple detection methods working in concert to provide high accuracy.

Our technology is built on extensive research in computer vision, deep learning, and digital forensics, making it one of the most advanced AI detection systems available.
